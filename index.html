Top three places to visit in Kizu!

Go for a hike at Gansenji Temple!

Gansenji Temple is a renowned hiking destination offering breathtaking views and a sea of vibrant hydrangeas in full bloom.

Try our local diner at Yamashiro!

Savor a diverse array of comfort foods, from Japanese and Indian to American classics, all under one diner.

Indulge in traditional sweets in Kamo!

Step into Komayasu, where you can try a neat selection of wagashi crafted by one of Kamo's wonderful families!
